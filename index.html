Key Futures Concepts

  • Margin & Leverage Futures require margin deposits, not full contract value. This provides leverage but amplifies both gains and losses.
  • Contract Specifications Each futures contract (ES, NQ, CL, GC) has specific tick sizes, point values, and trading hours. Know your contract.
  • No PDT Rule Unlike stocks, futures have no Pattern Day Trader rule. You can day trade with any account size.
